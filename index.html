Who we are?

Welcome to GCM School, a beacon of hope and education for young learners. Our dedicated institution is driven by a noble cause: to provide underprivileged children with a solid foundation in basic education. As we strive to create a brighter future for these children, we invite generous donors to join hands with us. Your support can make a profound impact, ensuring that every child receives a quality education and a chance at a better life. Join our mission at GCM School and be a part of transforming lives through education.

What we do?

Our school is a nurturing haven where children are guided on their first steps of learning. Through engaging courses in language, math, science, arts, and more, we pave the way for their academic growth. We go beyond the classroom, nurturing values of curiosity, empathy, and creativity. With a focus on inclusivity, we provide underprivileged children with the tools to build a brighter future and break down barriers that hinder their potential. At Sunshine Steps, we empower children to embark on a journey of discovery and promise, fostering the belief that education can transform lives.

Purpose

GCM school serves the purpose of providing free or subsidized education to underprivileged children who may not have access to proper schooling otherwise. These schools aim to bridge educational gaps, promote literacy, and empower disadvantaged communities by offering fundamental knowledge and skills, ultimately contributing to a more equitable and educated society.
